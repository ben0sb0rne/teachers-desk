The Teacher's Desk Marble Race How to

Marble Race

Every student is a marble. Pick a class, choose one of the six tracks on the playfield cards, then hit Launch (or Space). The whole class races the course and the finish order fills the board as marbles cross the line — first place is recorded in the shared call counts, same as a wheel spin.

Reset lays out a fresh course on the same track. Fullscreen (F) drops the chrome for the projector.
