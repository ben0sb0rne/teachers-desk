THE TEACHER'S DESK

About

A proper about page is coming soon.

The Teacher's Desk is a small suite of free, private classroom tools. Everything runs in your browser — no accounts, no tracking, no backend. Your data stays in this device's local storage; export it to a JSON file any time.

Built with care, slowly.
