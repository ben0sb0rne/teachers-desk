Team Maker

Pick a class, mark anyone absent today, then tell it how to split: Teams of N or N teams. The math offers the sensible layouts (say, 2 of 4 + 2 of 3) — pick one, choose a reveal style, and hit Make teams.

The reveal is the fun part: a marble sorter, dealt draft cards, a gacha machine you crank (or set to auto), or a terminal running a very serious algorithm. Space runs the reveal, Re-roll shuffles new teams, and the board on the right keeps the full names.

Teams are random every time. Nobody's feelings get hurt; blame the machine.
